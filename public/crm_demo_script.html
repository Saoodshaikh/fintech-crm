Sales Tool & Presentation Script

FinVest CRM — Project Head Demo Playbook

A sorted guide to presenting the CRM system to win new business commissions.

Use this guide during your client presentation. It is structured to focus on business value (what the CRM solves for them) and interactive wow-factors (to prove it is a living prototype, not a mock design).

Phase 1: The Pitch Hook

1
Setting the Context

Action

Open the browser to Dashboard page but do not interact yet. Look at the client directly.

What to Say

"As a Mutual Fund Distributor, your biggest asset is client trust, but your biggest bottleneck is operational overhead: tracking SIP dues, resolving KYC issues, and building custom client portfolios. We built the FinVest CRM to automate this completely, serving as a unified command center."

Phase 2: The Command Center

2
Live Ticker & Dashboard Metrics

Action

Hover over the Market Ticker at the top, then point out the 12 KPI cards and charts.

What to Say

"The second your advisors log in, they have live market feeds at the top. Below that, they see 12 dynamic KPIs like Total AUM, monthly commission book, and due today's SIP. It gives immediate transparency on business health."

Phase 3: The Climax (Interactive Wow Moment)

3
Mutual Fund Comparison & Analysis

Action

Navigate to Mutual Funds. Select checkboxes/compare buttons on two funds, click Compare Now on the bottom bar.

What to Say

"During customer calls, advisors can select up to 3 mutual funds, compare them side-by-side with overlapping return graphs, exit loads, and manager info, without switching tabs or tools."

4
Live Mandate Execution

Action

Click Invest on a fund. Choose client Rahul Sharma, select Lumpsum, input 50,000, click Proceed to Invest. Wait for success screen.

What to Say

"We integrated a live transaction drawer. We choose our client, input the amount, and proceed. The CRM simulates mandate processing through BSE Star MF/BillDesk, outputting a secure confirmation."

5
Dynamic Global State Verification

Action

Click Done. Navigate immediately to the Dashboard. Point at today's investment value.

What to Say

"Notice that today's investment metrics and transaction log updated globally. This is a fully responsive state-managed system. All changes propagate instantly."

Phase 4: Relationship & Operational Auditing

6
Client Profiles, KYC, & Timeline Logs

Action

Go to Clients, click on Rahul Sharma. Walk through the Overview, Portfolio, Documents, and Notes tabs.

What to Say

"Every client gets a digital folder. You see their nominee shares, document vault (PAN/Cheques), bank IFSC codes, and an audit trail timeline of their onboarding steps. Everything is ready for relationship managers."

Phase 5: Operational Utilities

7
Reports, Calendars & AI Assistant

Action

Open Reports (show PDF/Excel generation), go to Calendar (show event list), click the floating **AI Assistant**.

What to Say

"With one click, advisors export capital gain sheets or email client statements. The calendar tracks birthdays and scheduled portfolio reviews automatically. The assistant provides quick calculations on the go."

Phase 6: The Closing Pitch

8
Technical Advantages

Action

Open the **Settings** page and close the browser window when finished.

What to Say

"We built this using React, Vite, and TailwindCSS. The platform loads instantly, has zero backend dependencies for this front-end version, and is ready to hook into your broker APIs when you sign the contract."

💡 Presentation Secrets: Always emphasize that you have built a scalable framework. Highlight that the design system is modular, meaning they can request custom brand colors (like changing Navy Blue to their company's exact logo hex code) in less than 5 minutes.
